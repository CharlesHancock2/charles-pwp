Milestone 1

Purpose: This PWP will be a professional portfolio website that will be used to aid in job hunting. I intend to make the site as clean-looking as possible while also being functional and user-friendly.

Audience: This site will be designed for a professional audience in the tech community, such as recruiters and other web development professionals, who are typically involved in the hiring process.

Goal: The main goal of this site is to demonstrate my competency as a web developer to potential employers and/or recruiters. I plan to use the site as a starting point for my portfolio so I can update it with links to new projects as my abilities progress.

Persona

Name: Angela Smith

Role: Angela is the hiring manager for a mid-size tech company, she is looking to hire a junior developer with little experience.

Technology: An expert technology user with a CS degree. She uses a Linux PC at work but is familiar with Windows and Linux.

Behaviors and Attitudes: Angela is extremely busy and has multiple interviews most days of the week. She will typically spend a maximum of 5-7 minutes total reviewing an applicant's portfolio and resume. She prefers to use LinkedIn to review candidates because it provides a lot of information in one place, as well as links to portfolio websites.

Scenario: Angela would view my site in a situation where I have applied for an open position within her company and have the site linked through my resume/application. Typically she would view my website before speaking with me in order to make sure I was a good candidate.

Goals: She is looking to hire a junior developer with 1-3 years of experience. She needs to be able to determine whether someone is a good candidate or not within the first 5 minutes of reviewing their information.

Additional Info: I need to be able to use this website to determine whether or not to continue with an interview for this candidate.
